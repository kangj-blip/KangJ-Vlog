Title: 100 Different Questions to Stimulate the mind
---

When I was younger, I would wonder about reckless, frankly useless things, like "Why is Wi-Fi affected by harsh wind conditions?", and "When we use soap, does the soap get dirty?" Of course, the answer is no. 

But nowadays I find myself wondering about more existential, hopefully more thought-provoking questions about the mind, my relationships, nature, and self. 

Here are many such questions, where I will come back, update, answer, and delve deeper as time goes on.

1. What does it mean to truly know someone? At what point can I declare myself someone's true friend, or someone who understands them to the deepest of [levels](https://www.daniellebernock.com/what-it-means-to-know-someone-37/)? When the human mind can't understand the individualistic identity of who they themselves are, how is it possible to "know" others? How can I best allow others to "know" me?
I could know all the details of other people's lives, from where they were born to what their favourite car model is. But I am not satisfied with impersonal knowledge. It is all about [how we define "knowing"](https://thoughtcatalog.com/corinda-katz/2014/03/how-do-you-know-when-you-really-know-someone/).
How does this connect with celebrity culture in modern day, where people think they "know" their favourite celebrity to unsafe degrees?
2. How do we deal with Earth's existence in a chaotically "stable" system?
Do you know that the Earth's orbital period is 365.2422 days, meaning our basis of measuring the year is off by .2422 days? Do you know the system in which our society and Earth operates in, could be completely instable, and we would not know until it decides to go off our established and forever-assumed calculations? Afterall, what does stability mean? Can it ever be achieved? In the Carnian Pluvial Episode (CPE) roughly 234 to 232 million years ago during the Late Triassic Epoch, the Earth experienced a period of 1–2 million years of constant rain and high levels of humidity. Sounds like [chaotic era](https://three-body-problem.fandom.com/wiki/Chaotic_Era). And it happened on our Earth.
